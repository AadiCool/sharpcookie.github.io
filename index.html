BOOK AT A GLANCE

  • This book is a first of its kind attempt at providing concise solution to previous year question papers for the first year students of IIT Kharagpur.

  • This book contains 42 fully solved past year papers.

  • Key fomulae and key concepts are included to give the readers a boost and also to help them during the haste of revision.

  • It also comes with important tips and tricks that a student should adhere if aiming for a high cgpa.This tips have been jotted down by one of our author who himself had secured a 10 SGPA.

  • We believe if this book is throughly solved and the advices(fundae) metriculously followed then it would ensure a decent CGPA for the readers.
